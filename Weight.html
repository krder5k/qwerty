Get to workout!

You want powers off, fitness and team spirit?

"Get to workout" is a new high intesity team workout, trained exclusively with your own body weight.

The special feature is the distinctive aspect of the team: the team makes all exercises from start to finish together.

Our spirit: We do it together.

Interval mode, each individual limit, or beginners comes no matter whether super fit.

The instructor is part of the team and do all the exercises, he pays attention to the clean execution and motivated.

The combination of HIIT (high Intesitiy interval training) and full body workout brings the best fitness effect you with strength, strength endurance and athleticism in high fat burning during your workout and then.

Let's push you from the power of the team and "Get to work out"!

Be sure to bring along is an own sports mat for the floor exercises.
