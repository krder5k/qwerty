Basketball

Beginner - Learn the basic technical knowledge and the most important basic rules of the sport: play, catch, basket throw, two-contact rhythm and dribbling. Achieving game skills.

Advanced - improving technical skills. Achieving an improved level of play through targeted, positional training with the aim of participating in the game of sweepstakes.

Course goals:

Beginners - Getting technical fundamental knowledge and the most important basic rules of the game. Passing, catching, shooting, double contact rhythm and dribbling. Achievement of readiness to play.

Advanced - Improvement of technical skills. Achievement of an advanced game level by means of a purposeful, position controlled training with a view to participate in a competition.
